Custom Core Labs // Service Agreement & Limited Labor Warranty

1. Procurement & Payment Policy

Premium parts are ordered on your behalf after quote approval. For standard custom builds, component procurement is handled after client parts payment. For Signature Series Packages, orders are processed based on a guaranteed tier performance blueprint. Custom Core Labs retains complete authority over component supplier and brand selection behind the scenes to leverage strategic bundle discounts, provided all hardware meets or exceeds the technical performance metrics of the selected tier.

2. Payment & Package Lock Policy

Parts cost is collected prior to placing orders so Custom Core Labs can secure inventory and begin build preparation. The standard assembly labor fee is due only after the system is fully assembled, validated, and transferred at pickup. If an Express Priority Rush timeline is selected, the additional rush service fee must be funded 100% upfront alongside the parts procurement cost to cover immediate overnight air-freight logistics. Because dynamic hardware component market rates fluctuate daily, all custom invoices and package quotes are strictly valid for 48 hours from issuance. Accepted payment methods are strictly digital (Square) or cash upon pickup. Transactions processed via third-party credit card readers (Square) are subject to a standard 3% payment processing convenience fee and do not qualify for our cash/direct transfer pricing discounts.

3. Component Liability & Component Failure (DOA)

Custom Core Labs assumes zero financial or legal liability for components that arrive defective from a retailer for standard client-sourced builds. If a client-supplied component fails to pass hardware initialization (POST), Custom Core Labs will provide the exact diagnostic codes so the client can initiate a return with their retailer. For Signature Series Packages, Custom Core Labs assumes complete management of the vendor return and replacement workflow for any component found to be Dead on Arrival (DOA), securing a fresh replacement at no additional cost to the client to ensure the guaranteed tier performance metrics are met.

4. 90-Day Limited Labor Warranty

Custom Core Labs warrants the physical craftsmanship and assembly of the build for ninety (90) days from the date of handoff.

Covered: Loose cable connections, loose mounting hardware, or incorrect BIOS profiles applied by the technician. Diagnostic labor for issues occurring within 90 days is 100% free.

Not covered: Component hardware degradation, factory defects, user accidents (drops, liquid spills), user hardware modifications, internal liquid cooler (AIO) permeation or factory seal degradation, or software issues (malware, operating system corruption caused by the user).

5. Data Sovereignty & Privacy Policy

Client storage arrays are never duplicated, backed up, or manually reviewed. All operating system installations utilize official, unaltered distributions from Microsoft or open-source maintainers.
