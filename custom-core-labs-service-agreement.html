Custom Core Labs // Service Agreement & Limited Labor Warranty

1. Payment & Labor Policy

All payments are settled at pickup once work is complete. For custom builds, parts cost is collected before any order is placed, and the remaining labor and service fees are due at pickup. For diagnostic and repair services, the final amount is due upon pickup, after you have approved the work.

2. Diagnostic & Repair Framework

Full System Diagnostic — $45: The diagnostic service covers the bench time, specialized stress-testing, and expert labor required to accurately isolate a system issue. All payments are settled at pickup once the work is complete.

Integrated Solution Pricing: If a standard repair service is selected to fix the system, the diagnostic fee is completely absorbed into a single, combined solution price at checkout. The customer is never obligated to proceed with a repair and can choose to take the system back after receiving the diagnostic report.

Instant Resolutions: If the issue is permanently resolved entirely within the initial testing process (such as utilizing a physical BIOS flashback button, clearing CMOS, or resolving a loose cable connection), no additional repair fees are added. The customer is only billed the base $45 diagnostic rate.

Specialized Lab Procedures: For unique hardware scenarios that do not fall under standard website services (such as straightening bent motherboard socket pins), a single flat-rate labor quote will be provided for customer approval before any specialized work begins.

3. Component Liability & Component Failure (DOA)

Custom Core Labs assumes zero financial or legal liability for components that arrive defective from the retailer (Dead on Arrival / DOA). If a component fails to pass hardware initialization (POST) due to a factory defect, Custom Core Labs will provide the exact diagnostic codes so the client can initiate a return with the retailer (Amazon, Newegg, Best Buy, etc.).

4. 90-Day Limited Labor Warranty

Custom Core Labs warranties the physical craftsmanship and assembly of the build for ninety (90) days from the date of handoff.

Covered: Loose cable connections, loose mounting hardware, or incorrect BIOS profiles applied by the technician. Diagnostic labor for issues occurring within 90 days is 100% free.

Not covered: Component hardware degradation, factory defects, user accidents (drops, liquid spills), user hardware modifications, or software issues (malware, operating system corruption caused by the user).

5. Data Sovereignty & Privacy Policy

Client storage arrays are never duplicated, backed up, or manually reviewed. All operating system installations utilize official, unaltered distributions from Microsoft or open-source maintainers.
