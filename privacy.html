CUSTOM CORE LABS // PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: May 2026

At Custom Core Labs, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines exactly how we handle your information when you visit customcorelabs.pages.dev and use our intake forms.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when initiating a project request, including:

  • Personal Identifiers: Full Name, Email Address, and Phone Number.
  • Project Specifications: PCPartPicker links, component budgets, and specific hardware diagnostic/upgrade descriptions.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

Your information is strictly used to conduct business operations. We use it to:

  • Review your hardware lists and calculate labor costs.
  • Coordinate drop-off and pickup logistics at our Bushnell's Basin office.
  • Issue your unique Secure Pickup Code for secure hardware handoff.
  • Contact you regarding the status of your computer build or repair.

3. DATA RETENTION & SECURITY

We do not operate databases or cloud servers to store your personal files. Intake form submissions are processed securely through our platform hosting infrastructure (Cloudflare Pages). We enforce a strict "zero-retention" policy for client storage media — we never clone, back up, browse, or store data from your personal hard drives.

4. THIRD-PARTY DISCLOSURE & COOKIES

Custom Core Labs is a privately operated entity.

  • We do not sell, trade, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties.
  • We do not run tracking cookies or targeted marketing algorithms on our website.
  • Our site is hosted via Cloudflare Pages, which may collect basic, anonymous technical server logs (such as IP addresses and browser types) solely to maintain website uptime, security, and DDOS protection.

5. CONTACT INFORMATION

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or wish to request the complete deletion of your intake form communication data, please contact us via the official methods listed on our website or text our bench directly.
